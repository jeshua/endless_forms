Power Flow

Battery + (3.7V LiPo, ~4V charged) → F2 pad → J2 (Switch COM) → J3 (Switch OUT) → 6J (Battery Bus ~4V) → Splits:
  • 6A → 9A (XIAO power input)
  • 6G → 20G (LED ring 4V power)
Battery - → J7 (directly on GND Bus) → Splits:
  • 7B → 9B (XIAO GND)
  • 7A → 17A (10K resistor to GND)
  • 7F → 20F (LED ring GND)
XIAO 3V3 Output (regulated from ~4V input) → 9C → 20A (FSR power, 3.3V)

Signal Connections

FSR Sensor (Voltage Divider):
  • FSR + wire → 20A pad (3.3V from 9C)
  • FSR - wire → 20B pad → 16A junction
  • Junction 16A connects:
    • 15A (D0) reads voltage
    • 17A (10K to GND)
LED Ring:
  • LED DIN wire → 20E pad → 15B (D1)
  • LED GND wire → 20F pad (from GND bus)
  • LED 4V wire → 20G pad (from battery bus)

Critical Traces (Back of Board)

Power Buses (horizontal wires across A-J):
  • 6A-6J: Battery bus ~4V (solid wire across all 10 columns)
  • 7A-7J: GND bus (solid wire across all 10 columns)
Power Switch connections:
  • F2 → J2: Battery+ pad to switch COM pin ✓
  • J3 → 6J: Switch OUT pin to battery bus ✓
  • J7: Battery- wire directly on GND bus ✓
Bus to XIAO connections:
  • 6A → 9A: Battery bus to XIAO power
  • 7B → 9B: GND bus to XIAO ground
Component connections:
  • 16A → 15A: Junction to D0 (FSR reading)
  • 17A → 7A: 10K resistor to GND bus
  • 9C → 20A: 3V3 to FSR+ pad
  • 20B → 16A: FSR- pad to junction
  • 15B → 20E: D1 to LED DIN pad
  • 6G → 20G: Battery bus to LED 4V pad
  • 7F → 20F: GND bus to LED GND pad
